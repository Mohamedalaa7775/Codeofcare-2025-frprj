Our Best Services For
Your Solution

We provide these services sincerely for the sake of God to patients with chronic diseases,
as they enable them to live with their diseases naturally and make it easy for them
to deal with the diseases and not only be concerned for their families and live in peace,
security and health, God willing.

Calculating daily doses for diabetics

We can measure the daily doses for diabetics by knowing the patient's weight only, and thus we can provide him with a diet service for his meals , There is also a medication reminder service and an alert to take the prescribed medication

Predicting the possibility of a heart attack

Some elderly people and others suffer from heart diseases. We provide them with a specialized tool to predict the possibility of having a heart attack based on age, weight, blood glucose, and blood pressure.

Providing a diet service for people

Providing a complete diet service for patients with high blood pressure through Database for most foods, based on the percentage of nitrogen in them, because it affects patients with high blood pressure negatively.

Medical chatbot for all diseases

We offer a medical chatbot that can answer any question in the context of medicine only. It can also recommend medications for you and help you in emergency situations and cases of high and low blood sugar for diabetics.
